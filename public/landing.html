Galaxy Conquest

Welcome to Galaxy Conquest, a turn-based multiplayer space strategy game where you compete to dominate the galaxy! Originally built in 2012, Game of Worlds has been completely rewritten in Node.js with modern web technologies. This real-time turn-based strategy game combines the depth of classic 4X games with the accessibility of browser-based gaming.

Plan your moves carefully, manage your resources, research powerful technologies, and engage in tactical combat to expand your empire across the stars.

Key Features:

  • 12 Unique Races: Each with distinct abilities and playstyles.
  • Turn-Based Strategy: Plan your moves carefully in 3-minute turns.
  • Multiplayer Battles: Compete against other players in real-time.
  • Tech Tree: Research advanced technologies to gain an edge.
  • Resource Management: Balance metal, crystal, and research production.
  • Cross-Platform: Play in any modern web browser.
