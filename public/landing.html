// 01 — FIELD DOCTRINE

How the galaxy is won

Exploration is a gamble, not a formality. Every sector you enter can hand you a homeworld — or erase your fleet. This is the game's spine.

HAZARD

Explore at your peril

Unknown sectors hide planets, ore fields — and black holes that crush a fleet the instant it arrives. Scout with a 300-crystal probe, or gamble warships and pray the dark is empty.

CONTEST

Seize the hazards

An asteroid belt shreds ships on entry — random losses, every pass — until you own it. Secure the deadly sectors and turn death-traps into safe supply lanes no rival dares cross.

ECONOMY

Forge an empire

Metal Extractors, Crystal Refineries, Research Academies, Spaceports, Orbital Turrets, Warp Gates. Every world you hold becomes a factory for the war to come.

WARFARE

Command real fleets

Raid supply lines with fast interceptors; break worlds with orbital dreadnoughts. Read the enemy's doctrine and counter it with the right tech at the right turn.

TEMPO

Quick or Epic

Binge a whole campaign in twenty minutes of fast turns — or trade one decisive move a day with friends over weeks. Epic mode boosts income so every order lands hard.

AI CORE

Allies & foes on tap

Fill empty seats in seconds. Drill against an AI-only sandbox, or blend humans and bots so a match always fires — no waiting on a full lobby.

THEATRE OF WAR

The bridge is yours.
Give the order.

Capital ships hang over a burning frontier. Every turn, somewhere in the grid, a fleet commits and a world changes hands. Step onto the bridge and make the next move count.
